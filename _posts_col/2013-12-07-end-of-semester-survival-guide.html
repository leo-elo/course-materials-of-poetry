--- layout: post title: "End of Semester Survival Guide" date: 2013-12-07 slug: "end-of-semester-survival-guide" permalink: /end-of-semester-survival-guide/ categories: ["Assignments", "news"] --- As we wrap up the course, there are a few things we need to take care of:
  • Assignment Self-Evaluation
    • Write a 1-2 paragraph self-evaluation of your assignment work in the class, taking into consideration the following questions:
      • Did you do all the assignments?
      • Did you do them thoroughly and on time?
      • How well did you do them?
      • Be sure to mention any bonus assignments completed.
    • Print out your self-evaluation and turn it in the day of the final exam.
  • Final Exam
    • Read and study T. S. Eliot's "The Love Song of J. Alfred Prufrock." It's in our textbook and an online annotated version is available here.
    • Review everything assigned in this course.
    • Bring paper (ruled, blank, whatever you like).
