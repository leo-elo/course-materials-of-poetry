--- layout: post title: "Hello Students!" date: 2013-08-14 slug: "hello-students" permalink: /hello-students/ categories: ["news"] --- Welcome to our online space for this course. This blog will be our online base of operations, where I will post course information, assignments, and fun things related to our exploration of poetry. You’ll see links to the Syllabus, Calendar, and other course related materials. I encourage you to link to this page and visit it often. You can also subscribe to the blog via e-mail or RSS feed. We will also be using a Facebook page for the course, which will be our online discussion space. You will need a Facebook account and “like” the page so you can subscribe to its feeds. Feel free to use the Facebook page for whatever poetry related posting, photo, video, whatever you wish to share with the class. Don't hesitate to visit me at my office hours if you have questions about anything related to the course. Don't hesitate to contact me also by e-mail: my address is available in the syllabus and in this blog's sidebar. Through my professional blog, you’ll be able to see some of my research activities. I’m looking forward to exploring some splendiferous poetry with you this semester!
