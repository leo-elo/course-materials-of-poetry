--- layout: post title: "Bonus: Greeting Card Analysis" date: 2015-09-07 slug: "bonus-greeting-card-analysis" permalink: /bonus-greeting-card-analysis/ categories: ["bonus"] --- Here's a fun little exercise that you can do for extra credit.
  1. Go buy (or photograph) a greeting card with a poem in it.
  2. Analyze the poem's meter and rhyme.
  3. Write a brief (1-2 paragraphs) analysis and evaluation of the poem's use of rhythm, rhyme, and other sound patterns.
  4. Post it in our Facebook Group, including pictures of the postcard and poem.
